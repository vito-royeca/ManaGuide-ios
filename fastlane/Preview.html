Description
Mana Guide is probably the best card database for the most popular tradable and collectible card game, Magic: the Gathering.

Features:

- All Sets
Contains all sets from Alpha to the latest set. Foreign language cards are also included.

- Advanced Card Search
Find the specific cards you need with advanced filters.

- Card Pricing
View and track card pricing data.

- Card Rating
Rate cards and view the top rated
